Master Thesis

Shared Control of Smart Robot Wheelchairs

Using LiDAR sensors, the wheelchair can completely avoid collisions, ensuring user safety. In addition, a shared control algorithm for dual robotic manipulators was implemented. This innovative approach maps complex joint configurations to intuitive user inputs, leveraging YOLO, MoveIt, and intention estimation.

  • Hardware/CAN interfacing
  • ROS sensor integration
  • Navigation and dual arm manipulation systems developed
  • Docker containerization
Awards: Sir Bruce White Prize (Best Thesis in Electrical Engineering)

Human Centered Robotics Coursework

VR-Teleoperation: Quadraped Group (size: 10)

Avatour is a pioneering system that combines virtual reality (VR) with robotic teleoperation to provide immersive, real-world exploration experiences. Users can control a legged robot equipped with a 360-degree camera, navigating through various environments and interacting with their surroundings. This technology offers a unique way to explore distant places, whether due to physical limitations or a desire for adventure

  • Boston Dynamics' Spot robot with 360-degree camera for remote exploration
  • VR headset and tablet interfaces for immersive control
  • Live 360-degree video and environmental audio streaming
  • NVIDIA Jetson Orin for onboard processing and streaming
  • Containerized microservice architecture with custom GStreamer pipeline
  • Dual control modes with collision avoidance and safety features
  • WebRTC and Dynamic DNS for low-latency, reliable connectivity

Self Organising Multi-Agent Systems

Multi-agent simulator Group (size: cohort)

Developed a sophisticated self-organizing multi-agent system simulation called 'SOMAS World,' featuring a dynamic electronic environment where autonomous agents navigate complex survival scenarios. The project, centered around the 'Mega-Bike' concept, challenges agents to make strategic decisions, collaborate, and adapt in resource collection and threat avoidance. This simulation explores emergent behaviors, decision-making processes, and evolving social dynamics in a competitive yet cooperative setting, offering insights into collective intelligence and real-world social dilemmas.

  • Golang
  • Omstrom theory
  • Agentic dynamics
